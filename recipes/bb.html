Banana Bread

Description

Delicious banana bread. Great recipe for turning slightly overripe bananas into a treat!

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter
  • ½ cup Soft Brown Sugar (lightly packed)
  • ½ cup Caster Sugar
  • 2 bananas (ripe), mashed
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups Plain Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C bake. Grease and line a 25cm loaf pan.
  2. Beat Butter and sugars together until pale and creamy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  3. Sift in Plain Flour, Baking Powder, and cinnamon.
  4. Dissolve Baking Soda in Milk. Add to creamed butter mixture with bananas and walnuts. Mix together.
  5. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour or until a skewer comes out clean.
