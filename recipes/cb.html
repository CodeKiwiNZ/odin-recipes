Chocolate Brownies

Description

Make them and hide them before they mysteriously disappear. Amazing hot with vanilla icecream.

Ingredients

  • 250g Butter
  • ½ cup White Sugar
  • ½ cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cups Plain Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C bake. Line an 18 x 28cm sponge roll tin with baking paper.
  2. Melt Butter in a saucepan large enough to mix all ingredients in. Mix in cocoa, remove from heat and stir in White Sugar.
  3. Add eggs and mix well, then add vanilla essence. Sift in Plain Flour and Baking Powder and mix to combine. Pour into prepared tin.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until brownie springs back when touched lightly.
