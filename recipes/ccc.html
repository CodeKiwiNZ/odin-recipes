Choc Chip Cookies

Description

Simple and easy, these chocolate chip cookies are quick to make and taste great

Ingredients

  • 125g Butter
  • ½ cup Soft Brown Sugar (lightly packed)
  • ½ cup Caster Sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 egg
  • 1¾ cups Self Raising Flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup chocolate chunks or chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C conventional or 160ºC fan forced and grease or line two baking trays with baking paper.
  2. Beat Butter, Soft Brown Sugar, Caster Sugar and vanilla until pale and creamy, add egg and beat until mixed.
  3. Sift in Self Raising Flour and salt, mix until combined before stirring in the chocolate chunks or chips.
  4. Roll heaped teaspoon sized balls of mixture and place on tray, press down lightly with a floured fork. Bake for approx. 12 minutes.
